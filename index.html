Our Students

Our students are all those teachers who feel a deep commitment towards education and their own professional development. In our online school, they receive practical and effective help, support, and academic coaching according to their own needs. In order to provide effective and practical courses, we use as a reference the Cambridge Teaching Framework (CEFT) as well as our own life and experience as teacher trainers. Therefore, our online school offers a variety of courses designed according to the different stages of our professional life: from inexperienced to experienced teachers. You will always learn and improve your own teaching practice with us! See our Courses!.

Our courses!

We believe the professional development is a key area if teachers want to continue developing, improving and enjoying their daily practice.

We also understand that each teacher faces a unique challenge since the context is different and therefore, needs and wants are different in each one of our students. In order to make sure our courses are helpful, we make decisions taking these two principles into consideration:
1. You (as our student) are responsible for your own growth and you are capable of making your own decisions regarding your own learning and professional development.
2. Personalized formative feedback is essential to permanent growth for anyone interested in their professional development.
That is why we pay very close attention to your unique circumstances and use all our experience and knowledge to help you as much as we can.
